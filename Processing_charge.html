PROCESSING CHARGE

◙ FOR DEVELOPED AND DEVELOPING COUNTRIES

1. An Article and an Issue Charges of the Research Journal in which the paper will be published:US$440/-.

2. Charges for Additional Printed Page(s):Where an article exceeds Twelve Printed Pages (NOT TYPED PAGES), charges of US$11/- will be extra for each printed page.

3. Coloured Figure/Photographs: Processing for printing charges are US$110/- for each Figure/Photograph, whereas, if these are in Black and White there will be no charges (FREE)

4. An additional Issue Charges of the Research Journal in which the paper will be published: US$100/- (NOT MANDATORY).

◙ FOR UNDER / BACKWARD / LEAST-DEVELOPED COUNTRIES

1. An Article Charges: The Submission Charges, US$440/- can be waived off upto 75% or completely. Please write to the Administrative Editor (e-mail: editor@krepublishers.co.in) for availing the benefits.

2. Charges for Additional Printed Page(s): Where an article exceeds Twelve Printed Pages (NOT TYPED PAGES), charges of US$11/- will be extra for each printed page.

3. Coloured Figure/Photographs: Processing for printing charges are US$110/- for each Figure/Photograph, whereas, if these are in Black and White there will be no charges (FREE)

4. An Issue Charges of the Research Journal in which the paper will be published:US$100/- (NOT MANDATORY).

◙ FOR SAARC COUNTRIES

For the Processing Charges, Please write to the Administrative Editor (e-mail: editor@krepublishers.co.in)
